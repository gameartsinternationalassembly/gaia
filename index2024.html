GAME ARTS INTERNATIONAL ASSEMBLY

The Game Arts International Assembly will happen ONLINE on Tuesday Sept. 10th and Thursday Sept. 19th.

This time GAIA will host 12 sessions distributed in 2 days with over 20 curators, community organizers and cultural producers from 12 countries from around the world. We will cover a wide range of topics such as online curation and community caring to innovative editorial approaches. In addition to talks sharing personal experiences, playful workshops, and practical sessions, GAIA will include an online artgames showcase. Sessions will take place via private Zoom meetings. Those interested in attending are required to sign up by filling out this form.
