GAME ARTS INTERNATIONAL ASSEMBLY

In September, the Game Arts International Assembly (GAIA) will host an online edition on Tuesday, September 10th, and Thursday, September 19th. This year, GAIA will feature 8 tracks spread across 2 days, with over 20 curators, community organizers, and cultural producers from 12 countries around the globe. The event will explore a diverse range of topics, including online curation, community care, and innovative editorial approaches. Alongside talks that share personal experiences, participants can engage in playful workshops, practical sessions, and an online art games showcase. Sessions will take place via private Zoom meetings. Those interested in attending are required to sign up by filling out this form.

Supporting local game scenes as community organizers

18:00 - 18:45 (45m)
David Antognoli + Emily Koonce + Seb Galvez + Host: Sol Sanchez
As organizers, we seek to cultivate and support our local game scenes. This conversation will give participants an opportunity to enumerate the needs of their local game scenes, the challenges they face, and the resources they thrive on. What strategies might help us connect these resources to our game scenes? We seek to provide a venue for potential and current organizers to share their experience and actionable steps that have worked for them.
